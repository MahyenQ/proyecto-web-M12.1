Social Action Programs

Education

We help girls, boys, teenagers, and young people continue their studies so they can have a future with more opportunities.

Training and Employment

We offer training opportunities to young people so they can improve the skills needed to access a job.

Shelter Centers

We support minors and young people under or formerly under guardianship on their path toward an autonomous and independent life.

Values Education

We promote youth solidarity commitment to foster their critical thinking and social activism.

Prevention of Excessive Technology Use

We use the digital environment to educate young people on healthy tech use and reduce abusive habits.

Adults

We develop projects and activities to promote the employment integration of people over 30.
