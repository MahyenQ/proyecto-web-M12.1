WHAT IS AMPAZ?

It is dedicated to equality and equity rights in migration and refugee status, which seek to ensure that all people, regardless of their origin, are treated with respect and have equal opportunities. Equality means that everyone should be treated equally under the law, and equity means providing more help to those in need. It is also important to avoid the rejection or mistreatment of migrants. Defending these rights contributes to a more just society for all. It is a non-profit organization that works to help people who cannot return to their country of origin for reasons of safety, persecution, or violence, as well as people seeking a better life in another country. Its goal is to ensure that these organizations have access to basic services, such as healthcare, education, and legal support.

AMPAZ - REFUGEE AID

LEGAL AID TEAM

This group ensures that these people understand their rights and receive assistance with important paperwork, such as applying for asylum or permits to stay. They would also defend them if they encounter legal problems or if their rights are not respected.

INTEGRATION TEAM

This group helps them feel part of the community. They teach them the language, help them find work and a place to live, give them tools to help them support themselves, and teach them about the local culture and customs.

AWARENESS TEAM

This group is dedicated to helping local people understand why these people come and the importance of treating them well. They hold talks and activities to help us all become more empathetic and help people understand that these people have rights.

COLLABORATE

This group studies the problems these people face, gathers information, and proposes ideas to help laws and authorities better help them. They talk to politicians and other organizations to achieve important changes.
