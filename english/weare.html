Who are we?

Join us in ensuring that people, regardless of where they come from, are treated with respect and have equal opportunities.
Equality means that everyone should be treated equally under the law, and equity means giving more help to those who need it.
It's also important to avoid rejecting or mistreating migrants.
Defending these rights helps build a "fairer society for all."


Mission

We build a fairer, more supportive and inclusive society where diversity is embraced as a value and every person can pursue their life project.

We believe in each person’s capacity for change and we commit ourselves to the most vulnerable through closeness and support.


VALUES



Every person matters

At Ampaz, we believe in the unique value of each individual.
We put people at the center, accompanying them along their journey while recognizing their dignity and potential to grow.


No one left out

We believe everyone's participation matters.
We promote spaces where all voices are heard, valued, and considered in social change processes.


Together is better

We foster relationships based on respect, listening, and trust.
We believe that encounters between different people lead to more human and caring coexistence.


Justice that shows

We take action against any form of injustice, exclusion, or violence.
We advocate for equal treatment and opportunities, especially across all genders.


We dream, then we act

We imagine a world where justice and equity are real.
We work locally, with our eyes on a more sustainable, just, and hopeful shared future.


Clear and upfront

We are committed to transparency and honesty.
We share our work with clarity, coherence, and commitment to society.
