At Ampaz, we walk alongside people, regardless of their origin, so they are treated with dignity and have access to the same opportunities. We defend equality before the law and equity as a form of justice: giving more to those who need it most. We reject all forms of discrimination, especially against migrants, and work for a society where everyone can live with respect and hope.


Our program offers:
  • Legal advice on immigration: to help understand and exercise their rights.
  • Support for voluntary return: for those who decide to return to their country of origin.
  • Co-development projects: that encourage active participation in their home communities.
  • Awareness and advocacy actions: to promote a fair and humane view of migration.





Additionally, AMPAZ holds an annual Volunteering Gathering so that the people who collaborate with the organization can share their experiences and proposals in favor of refugees. These gatherings bring together volunteers from all delegations. They have become a unique and enriching experience, as well as an excellent training, motivating, and energizing space that enables discussion, mutual understanding, and exchange of ideas.


How to become a volunteer?



To access AMPAZ's volunteer program, it is necessary to attend an information session where you will learn about our work and the current volunteer needs.


Information sessions are held in IN-PERSON and VIRTUAL FORMAT.


You can book your spot in one of our information sessions to learn how to collaborate with AMPAZ and support refugees and migrants.
