WHAT DO WE DO?

Our organization (NGO) is called AMPaz, and its main mission is to promote peace and social justice. We are dedicated to defending the rights to equality and equity for all people who migrate or seek refuge. This means we work to ensure that every individual, regardless of their origin, is treated with dignity and respect, and that they have the same opportunities to thrive.

Equality is a fundamental principle for us, which means that everyone must be equal before the law, without discrimination. Equity, on the other hand, recognizes that some people may need more support than others to achieve equality, and that is why we strive to provide additional help to those who need it most.

In addition, we are committed to fighting rejection and mistreatment of migrants and refugees. We firmly believe that defending these rights is essential to building a fairer, more inclusive, and prosperous society for all its members.
