WE ANSWER YOUR QUESTIONS:
WHY DONATE YOUR MONEY TO OUR NGO?


At AMPAZ, we provide shelter for refugees in our shelters, as well as legal assistance, psychological support, training, and guidance for their social and employment integration.
In addition, we actively work on reporting and public advocacy to defend their rights, with the goal of promoting legislative changes that guarantee lasting improvements in their quality of life.

Making a donation means making a one-time contribution, at the time you decide.
It's a supportive way to collaborate with AMPAZ, especially useful in emergency situations or when immediate support is needed.
Becoming a member implies an ongoing commitment to our cause. It is a regular contribution (monthly, quarterly, annually, etc.) that allows us to better plan our actions and ensure the sustainability of our programs supporting migrants and refugees.
There is no minimum amount, every contribution counts.

Your donation allows AMPAZ to carry out concrete actions such as:

  • Accommodation: Maintenance and management of reception centers for migrants and refugees.
  • Legal advice: Legal support for regularization and defense of rights.
  • Psychological care: Emotional support and assistance in adaptation processes.
  • Training and job placement: Courses, workshops and guidance to facilitate integration.
  • Incidence and awareness: Campaigns for equality, equity, and human rights.

Every contribution, large or small, directly contributes to improving lives and building a more inclusive society.
